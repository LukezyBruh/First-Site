About the character

While walking through the streets of Hanamizuki, it's hard not to notice a young oni named Arataki Itto. No matter how his horns and loud voice stand out, just in the form of how enthusiastically he plays with the kids, it's enough not to confuse him with anyone else. And among the busy artisans of Hanamizaki, his idleness is too striking. Itto calls himself "The first and greatest leader of the famous Hanamizaka Arataka gang." He tried to explain the gang's activities to the employees of the Tenre commission who came to him about the investigation of a decent street fight, but his two-minute tirade was interrupted and summed up with a one-word entry: "Unemployed." This characterization is not entirely accurate: in addition to gang cases not recognized by the shogunate, Itto also takes various small jobs to make ends meet, working steadily for one day and resting all three. Therefore, it would be more correct to write "Three-quarters unemployed".
